About ORON

Providing hope and opportunity to vulnerable children since 1996

Our History & Mission

Founded in 1996 by Mr. and Mrs. Mwangi, ORON began as a response to the alarming levels of child neglect in Ongata Rongai. What started as a small feeding program for five children has grown into a comprehensive organization serving hundreds of children annually.

Our vision is to produce transformed and educated children who live with hope and opportunity for a better future in a world where residents of Ongata Rongai live without poverty.

Our mission is to work with children from disadvantaged backgrounds to eradicate poverty through education, mentorship and counselling, making a positive difference in their lives and contributing to the community's sustainable development.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our work and decision-making

Compassion

We approach every child with empathy, understanding their unique circumstances and treating them with dignity and respect.

Protection

We create safe environments where children are protected from harm, abuse, and exploitation.

Education

We believe education is the most powerful tool for breaking cycles of poverty and transforming lives.

Community

We work collaboratively with local communities to create sustainable solutions for vulnerable children.

Our Programs

Holistic services designed to meet the diverse needs of children in our care

Education Support

Providing scholarships, school supplies, and after-school tutoring to ensure our children receive quality education from pre-school through university.

Empowerment & Mentorship

Guiding children to make better life decisions, enhancing self-esteem, and developing character for success through leadership and life skills.

Safe Space & Platform

Providing a nurturing environment with library, recreational facilities, and shelter for orphans and abused children.
